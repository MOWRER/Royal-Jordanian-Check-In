Royal Jordanian Check In: Everything You Need to Know

Traveling with Royal Jordanian? The airline offers a smooth and flexible check-in experience designed for both leisure and business travelers. Whether you're flying domestically or internationally, Royal Jordanian provides multiple check-in options to save time and reduce airport stress. Here's a detailed breakdown of the Royal Jordanian check-in process, including online check-in, airport counters, mobile check-in, and self-service kiosks.

Online Check-In with Royal Jordanian

One of the easiest and most convenient ways to check in is via Royal Jordanian’s online check-in. Available through the official website or mobile app, online check-in opens 24 hours before departure and closes 4 hours before the flight for most international destinations. This option allows passengers to confirm their flight, select or change seats, and add extra baggage if needed. You can also download and print your boarding pass or save it to your mobile device—saving time at the airport.

Mobile Check-In with the Royal Jordanian App

Travelers who prefer using smartphones can check in using the Royal Jordanian mobile app, available on Android and iOS. The app offers seat selection, baggage updates, and digital boarding pass generation. You can also receive flight notifications, gate details, and baggage claim updates on your phone—ideal for travelers on the go.

Airport Counter Check-In

If you prefer in-person service or require special assistance, Royal Jordanian airport check-in counters open about 3 hours before international flights and 2 hours before domestic flights. Check-in typically closes 45 to 60 minutes prior to departure. It’s best to arrive early to ensure a smooth process through check-in and security.

Self-Service Kiosks

At select airports, Royal Jordanian self-service kiosks allow passengers to check in quickly and efficiently. These kiosks support seat selection, baggage check-in, and boarding pass printing. They’re especially helpful for passengers without checked baggage.

Baggage Check and Travel Tips

If you're checking in luggage, arrive early enough to complete your check-in and baggage drop. For international flights, baggage drop closes 60 minutes before departure, and for domestic flights, at least 45 minutes prior. Always confirm your baggage allowance and policies based on your destination and ticket type.

Passengers traveling with oversized luggage, sports equipment, or pets should arrive even earlier to ensure proper handling. If you need special assistance, such as wheelchair access or medical services, notify the airline in advance and check in early for dedicated support.

Contact and Customer Support

If you encounter issues during check-in—online, via the app, or at the airport—contact Royal Jordanian’s customer service for help. Their support team assists with check-in issues, booking changes, and baggage concerns. Call +1-866-551-1186 for real-time travel support from the U.S.

Final Tip

To enjoy a hassle-free Royal Jordanian check-in experience, confirm your flight details, arrive early, and utilize the digital tools available. Whether you check in online, through the mobile app, or at the airport, Royal Jordanian is committed to delivering a smooth and professional service every step of the way.
